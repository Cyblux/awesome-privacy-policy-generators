## Cookie Consent & Banners

* **[Klaro!](https://github.com/KIProtect/klaro)** - A beautiful, open-source, and privacy-compliant consent manager. It is lightweight, customizable, and easily integrates with any web stack.
* **[Osano Cookie Consent](https://www.osano.com)** - A popular open-source cookie consent popup creator. Good for basic compliance setups.
* **[Cookiebot](https://www.cookiebot.com)** - Automated cookie scanning and consent management, though the free tier drops off quickly based on your page count.

## Privacy-First Analytics

* **[Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io)** - Lightweight, open-source, and cookie-free web analytics. Fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR out of the box.
* **[Umami](https://umami.is)** - A self-hosted, open-source alternative to Google Analytics. Extremely fast, privacy-focused, and doesn't collect any personally identifiable information.
* **[Fathom Analytics](https://usefathom.com)** - Simple, clean, and reliable privacy-first analytics designed to handle millions of visitors without tracking individuals.
